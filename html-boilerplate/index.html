The strong element makes text bold. It makes readers aware that the text is important. The tone of voice on some screen readers will change to communicate the importance of the text with the strong tag. The strong tag can be used on its own similar the the paragraph tag.

This is an example of the strong tag.

The em element makes text italic. It places emphasis on the text in a different manner than strong. In some cases it may be used to convey unspoken thoughts or sarcasm. Em can be used on its own as well.

Indenting elements wtihin other elements is called nesting. When we nest elements we create a parent/child relationship between them. The nested elements are the children. We have used nesting so far to create this web page.

Comments are not visible in the browser, and are used to make future additions easier for other developers or our future selves. It gives us a point to jump off from with what we were trying to accomplish.
